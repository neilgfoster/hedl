## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-28

Initial release. Hedl is available as an Agent Skill with three opt-in adoption tiers (gate / lightweight / team) via `install.py`. The deterministic completion gate (`am_i_done.py`) and adversarial review panel are the headline capabilities. See `README.md` and `docs/getting-started.md`.
